About the Brand

A Story of
Timeless Luxury

Velouré is more than a fragrance — it is an intimate expression of identity. Each scent is composed with rare ingredients and meticulous artistry, designed to leave a trail of elegance wherever you go.

Our vision is to become a globally recognized luxury perfume house that defines modern elegance and emotional connection through scent. Every bottle is a promise of presence, confidence, and distinction.
